How to make Mouth Watering Wings

Have you ever wondered how they make those mouth watering amazing wings?

What is there secret? is there a special spice? Well today your going to find out!

Ingrediants

  • Fresh Chicken Wings
  • Butter
  • Salt and pepper
  • Garlic powder
  • Paprika

Directions

  1. Step 1

    2. Melt a stick of butter in a large bowl

  2. Step 2

    3. Toss your wings in the melted butter. Make sure to coat them well

  3. Step 3

    4. Sprinkle your wings with your spices. Dont over do it but just a dash of each.

  4. Step 4

    5. Preheat oven to 400

  5. Step 5

    6. Arrange your wings on a baking sheet. Place in oven and bake for 40 minutes then remove from oven.

  6. Step 6

    7. Add your favorite sauces and enjoy your delicious crispy wings!
