How to make a amazing cheese burger

you ever had a cheese burger that just melts in your mouth?

or a burger that you will always remember? well today we are going to learn how to make one of these beautiful burgers.

Ingrediants

  • 1 Lb of ground beef
  • 1 cup bread crumbs
  • 1 egg
  • tablespoon fresh garlic(or a couple cloves)
  • 2 tablespoon butter
  • onion powder
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese

Now that we have the ingrediants out of the way lets get to the fun part shall we?

Directions

  1. Step 1

    2. Take ground beef and put it in a large bowl. Add in the bread crumbs and egg and mix until blended well.

  2. Step 2

    3. Once mixed well mix in spices, melted butter and shredded cheddar cheese. Mix Well

  3. Step 3

    4. start forming chunks of meat into pattys. Try and make them about 1/2 in thick. Once all pattys are made, bbq to your desired wellness and enjoy!
