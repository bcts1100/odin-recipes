How to make amazing alfredo

do you want to make the best alfredo known to man?

well today is your lucky day. Today we will learn how to make a delicious chicken fettucine alfredo.

Ingrediants

  • 1 lb of chicken
  • seasonings of choice
  • 1 pack of fettucine noodles
  • 1 jar of cheesy alfredo sauce ( or make your own Here)

Steps

  1. step 1

    2. begin by browning your chicken and boiling a pot of water.

  2. Step 2

    3. once your water is boiling and your chicken is nice and browned add noodles to the water.

  3. step 3

    4. stir noodles until they are nice and done. then drain

  4. Step 4

    5. add sauce into a small saucepan and warm up

  5. step 5

    6. once all ingrediants are cooked and warm mix all together and enjoy!
